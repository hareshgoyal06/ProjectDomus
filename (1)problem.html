Canada plans to accept 500,000 immigrants by 2025.

As an immigrant coming to a foreign land - and a foreign way of life - obstacles often present themselves. Affordable housing is often a core need. However, a number of factors prevent immigrants from accessing housing. Whether it be unstable low wage jobs, or language barriers, or lack of knowledge/resources. For immigrants, having a social support system and proper resources can make THAT much of a difference. Through Project Domus, we hope to provide solutions to help immigrants not find just a house, but a home.

18% of immigrants and 27% of refugee-led households are in core housing need.

Often, this is the result of a lack of support and/or resources.
